# Report Formats

## Weekly client report (every Monday, <250 words)

> **Subject:** Your AI employee this week — {HEADLINE_NUMBER}
>
> **{HEADLINE_NUMBER}** — {one sentence on why it matters this week}
>
> **What your AI employee did:**
> - {count} × {task type tied to their stated success metric}
> - {count} × {task type}
> - {count} escalated to you / handled by you
>
> **Needs your input:** {item or "Nothing this week."}
>
> **Improving next:** {the one improvement in flight}
>
> {OPERATOR_NOTE}

Rules: plain language; no jargon; never inflate; omit a section rather than
estimate a missing metric; vary the headline week to week.

## Monthly value summary (Core/Embedded)

> **This month:** {work delivered, counts by workflow}
> **Estimated impact:** {$ figure} — based on your numbers: {client's lead/customer value, cited}
> **Trend:** {vs. last month}
> **Improvement delivered:** {what changed this month}
> **Next month:** {planned improvement}

## Proactive incident notice (client-visible failures only)

> {NAME} — flagging something before you'd notice it: {what happened, one
> sentence}. Impact: {scope}. It's {fixed / being fixed}, and we've added
> {prevention} so it doesn't recur. Nothing needed from you.

Send within 4 business hours of a client-visible error. The client must never
discover a failure before we tell them.
